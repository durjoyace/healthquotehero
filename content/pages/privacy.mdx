# HealthQuoteHero.com Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 14, 2026**

We appreciate your trust and take care ensuring your personally identifiable information is kept safe. HealthQuoteHero.com ("We", "Us", "Service") and its websites ("Services") will only use said information for purposes disclosed in this privacy policy.

## Information We Collect and Use

We collect personal information when you use Our Service, including:

- Name and contact information (phone number, email address)
- Date of birth and ZIP code
- Health-related information you provide (for matching you with appropriate plans)
- Household size and income information (for subsidy calculations)

This information is used to identify health insurance products and services that fit your profile and connect you with licensed insurance agents who can assist you.

### Automatic Data Collection

Additionally, this Service collects data for the following purposes:

- **Analytics**: To understand how users interact with our site and improve our services
- **Remarketing**: To show relevant advertisements on other websites you visit
- **Heat Mapping**: To display areas of pages where users most frequently interact

## How We Share Your Information

When you submit your information, we may share it with:

- **Licensed Insurance Agents**: To provide you with quotes and enrollment assistance
- **Insurance Carriers**: To facilitate plan enrollment
- **Service Partners**: Third-party partners who assist in our operations

We do NOT sell your personal information to data brokers or unrelated third parties.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Email Communications
When submitting your email to us, you are registering to receive communications from us directly and from our third-party service partners. If you no longer wish to receive these communications, please click on the unsubscribe link in our emails.

### Opt-Out Rights
You may request to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your personal information
- Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, email us at support@healthquotehero.com.

### California Residents (CCPA)
California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to request deletion.

## Third Party Websites

When you click from our site to another, please keep in mind you will be interacting with a third party and not governed under our terms of service or privacy policy. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:
- Improve the quality of our service
- Track usage statistics to understand how people interact with our site
- Remember your preferences

Cookies do not collect personally identifiable information, and we do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information. You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser, though some features may not work properly.

We may also place tracking pixels provided by third-party partners to measure advertising effectiveness.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

- 256-bit SSL encryption for data transmission
- Secure data storage practices
- Access controls limiting who can view your information

## Children's Privacy

This Website is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We will not knowingly collect any personal information from visitors under the age of 18. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to Our Privacy Policy

If we change our privacy policy, we will post changes on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of our site after changes indicates your acceptance unless you notify us otherwise.
